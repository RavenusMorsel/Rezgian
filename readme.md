# Rezgian

Rezgian is a quiet, atmospheric, story‑driven online world inspired by the collaborative spirit of early internet communities.  
It is designed to feel alive, modular, and expandable — a place where rooms, characters, and stories grow over time.

This project is built with clarity, structure, and long‑term maintainability in mind.  
Every system is designed to be modular, testable, and easy to extend as the world evolves.

---

## 🌍 Premise

Rezgian is a realm of small stories and quiet magic — a world where players explore rooms, interact with NPCs, and uncover lore at their own pace.  
It is not a fast‑paced MMO; it is a **living chat‑based RPG world** with:

- atmospheric locations  
- persistent characters  
- modular systems  
- a focus on community and narrative  

The goal is to recreate the feeling of early collaborative online worlds, but with modern tools and clean architecture.
